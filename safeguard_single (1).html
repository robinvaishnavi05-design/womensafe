🧠 ARCHITECTURE

How SafeGuard AI Works

01
📡

Data Streams

GPS coordinates, accelerometer readings, time-of-day, nearby incident reports and SOS history stream into the engine every 5 seconds.

02
🤖

ML Risk Scoring

A weighted anomaly model fuses all signals into a 0–100 risk index. Each factor carries a tuned weight based on historical incident patterns.

03
⚡

Smart Alerting

Score >60 → silent ping to trusted contacts. Score >80 → emergency SMS. Score >90 → auto-dial nearest police helpline.

04
🗺️

Safe Route Engine

Real-time alternatives route around flagged areas, prefer lit streets, and prioritise proximity to police stations and hospitals.
